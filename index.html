Alaska Frequently Asked Questions

Is it completely dark during the winter months?

No, but the amount of light depending on where you are will vary substantially.
Anchorage will have 5.5 hours of daylight on Dec 21, and Fairbanks will have about 3.75 hours.

How big are the moose?

A male moose (bull) can be as tall as 9 feet and weigh 1600 pounds!

What is the capital of Alaska

Juneau, and it's only accessible by boat or plane.

Are there really no snakes in Alaska?

Correct! There are no snakes in Alaska nor lizards.

How tall is Denali (Mt McKinley)?

Denali, the tallest peak in N. America, is 20,310 feet (6,190 meters) above sea level.
